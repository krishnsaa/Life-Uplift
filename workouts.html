Steps for a Basic Workout

  1. Warm up with light cardio for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Perform a series of bodyweight exercises.
  3. Rest for 30-60 seconds between exercises.
  4. Repeat the circuit 2-3 times.
  5. Cool down with stretching for 5-10 minutes.

Types of Bodyweight Workouts

Push-Up Workout

  1. Standard Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  2. Wide-Grip Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  3. Diamond Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  4. Incline Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  5. Pike Push-Ups: 10-15 reps

Leg Workout

  1. Squats: 15-20 reps
  2. Lunges: 10-15 reps per leg
  3. Bulgarian Split Squats: 10-15 reps per leg
  4. Glute Bridges: 15-20 reps
  5. Calf Raises: 15-20 reps

Core Workout

  1. Plank: Hold for 30-60 seconds
  2. Bicycle Crunches: 15-20 reps per side
  3. Leg Raises: 15-20 reps
  4. Russian Twists: 15-20 reps per side
  5. Mountain Climbers: 20-30 reps per side

Full-Body Workout

  1. Burpees: 10-15 reps
  2. Jump Squats: 15-20 reps
  3. Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  4. Plank: Hold for 30-60 seconds
  5. High Knees: 30-60 seconds

Advantages of Bodyweight Exercises

  • Push-Up Workout: Strengthens upper body and core.
  • Leg Workout: Builds lower body strength and endurance.
  • Core Workout: Enhances core stability and strength.
  • Full-Body Workout: Provides a comprehensive workout targeting multiple muscle groups.
